Task 7: Ways to Archive Online Videos

Archiving videos online is a simple process of searching up a YouTube downloader. First, select/click on the video you would like to save onto your hard drive. Next, copy the URL of the video you would like to salvage and paste it into the website “keepvid.com” After the website generates the video to become downloadable, pick the format you want the video in (preferably “MP4”) and click download. Once the download completes, you have an official, movie file copy of a YouTube video.
